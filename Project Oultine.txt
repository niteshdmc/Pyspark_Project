Project Outline:

What we are going to do:-
1. Data volume of about 15GB per day generated by Transactional System (like Billing Counter)
2. Read data from S3
3. Data Transformation
4. Writing data to DataMart


Why we doing it: 
1. Business wants to incentivise (Give extra money) the billing person(sales guy) based on their total sales.
2.Customer spending is tracked and based on the data coupon/discount is offered.
3. Daily Report
4. Monthly Report

How to achieve that:
1. S3 boto3 used to connect with S3
2. Spark used for Data Transformation
3. Transformed data pushed to Datamart for reporting process.
